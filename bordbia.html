bord bia.

Origin Green.

date. feb 2018

This was a live projects for Bord Bia, the Irish Food Board. The brief was to design a window display for Origin Green which is Ireland’s food and drink sustainability programme. I produced a motion piece for the window. People passing could potentially watch the full thing but realistically weren’t going to, so I wanted the individual elements and information to make sense by itself and give a sense of Origin Green.

The basis of Origin Green is to produce sustainably. I used the circle throughout my design to represent this and to compliment the circular Origin Green logo that already existed.

Origin Green already has a distinct visual language so I wanted to compliment this rather than challenge it.
