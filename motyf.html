motyf.

motion type festival.
Date. feb 2018

This was a live project for MOTYF 2018. MOTYF is an international festival that explores type in motion. I wanted my visual language to showcase the fluidity and malleability of type. MOTYF is an international festival that explores type in motion.

I wanted to demonstrate how typography is diverse and can be manipulated. To do this incorperated hands into my design and made the type feel fluid.
