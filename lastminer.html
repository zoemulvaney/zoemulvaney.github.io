last miner.

the last miner.

film title.
date. may 2018

The last miner is a documentary by Luke Brabazon that follows the life of Ireland's Last Miner, Jimmy Power. Jim Power, the last man left working in his father’s failing coal mine. There is no coal in the mine and Jimmy spends his days mining in vain. His efforts are futile but it's the only life he knows.

To create the visual langauge I wanted to pare back the mining experience to the most basic senses that a miner would experience. So I focused on light and sound.
