Palestine.

palestine.

date. feb 2019

I chose to look at Palestine in relation to the “Lost” brief. Not only have they lost land but their have also lost their basic human rights and human necessities.

I used Maslows heirarchy of needs to highlight how the Palestinian’s aren’t even making the bottom tier of human needs such as health, shelter, food, water, sleep, property, social stability, employment, clothing and so on. I made the chapters in my book based on these losses. The chapters are; displacement, detainment, health care and deaths..

The book is trilingual. It’s primarily English but uses hebrew and arabic throughout. The journal is for a Western audience so hebrew and arabic were not as dominant as english.
